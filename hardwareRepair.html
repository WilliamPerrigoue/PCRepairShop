Green River College PC Repair Shop

Is your computer experiencing functional issues due to a bad battery or a broken screen? Is your keyboard missing keys? Does your computer turn off randomly? We're here to help!

Our hardware repair services cover a wide range of issues, with our most common repairs including:

  • Screen Replacements
  • Battery Replacements
  • CPU Replacements
  • Hard Drive/Solid State Drive Replacement
We also offer services for more complex hardware issues, such as motherboard replacements and cooling system repairs to ensure your device stays at a temperature that is safe for both your computer's functionality, but also your home itself. Our technicians are skilled in diagnosing and repairing hardware problems, ensuring your device runs smoothly again. If you have a hardware issue, please contact us to schedule a repair appointment.
