Green River College PC Repair Shop

The Green River College PC Repair Shop is a place where students learn the skill of repairing the physical components and the software of computers and laptops. Its daily operations are conducted and maintained by those very same students. The Green River College PC Repair Shop is dedicated to providing hands-on experience and practical knowledge to aspiring technicians, who are highly motivated individuals that truly enjoy and have a passion for what they do.

    **Other requests on a case by case basis**

    While the Green River College PC Repair Shop is a student-run operation. It is important to note that the shop may not have the capacity to handle every request. For all broken parts and surrendered computers, we dispose of responsibly through an E-cycling center. These centers will accept any and all electronic devices/accessories and dispose of them accordingly. These centers are in partnership with the state of Washington and offer this service to educational institutions at no cost to the school. For more information about E-cycling, please visit the official Washington state E-cycle Washington website. This recycling service is also free of charge to the public, so if you have any old electronics that you want to recycle, pay one of the E-cycling sites a visit!

    Special requests will be approved subject to the PC Repair Shop Faculty Advisor's discretion. For any additional information, please contact the shop at pcrepairshop@greenriver.edu or at 253-931-6579.
