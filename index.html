Green River College PC Repair Shop

The Green River College PC Repair Shop is a place where students learn the skill of repairing thephysical components and the software of computers and laptops. Its daily operations are conducted and maintained by those very same students. The Green River College PC Repair Shop is dedicated to providing hands-on experience and practical knowledge to aspiring technicians, who are highly motivated individuals that truly enjoy and have a passion for what they do.

Services Offered

The Green River College PC Repair Shop offers a wide range of services to meet the needs of our customers. Our services include:

    Hardware Repair

    • Screens
    • Keyboards
    • Power Supplies
    • Motherboards
    • Hard Drives
    • Memory (RAM)
    • Cooling Systems
    • Graphics Cards
    • Network Cards
    • Battery Replacement

    Software Installation

    • Operating System Installation
    • Driver Installation
    • Software Updates
    • Antivirus Installation
    • System Configuration
    • Application Installation

    Computer Building

    • Custom PC Builds
    • Upgrades
    • Performance Optimization
    • System Assembly

    Data Services

    • Malware Remediation
    • Data Recovery
    • Data Backup and Transfer
  • Other requests on a case by case basis

    While the Green River College PC Repair Shop is a student-run operation, it is important to note that the shop may not have the capacity to handle every request. Special requests will be subject to the shop facilitator's discretion. For any additional information, please contact the shop at pcrepairshop@greenriver.edu or at 253-931-6579.
